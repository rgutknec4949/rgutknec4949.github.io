Wells Fargo

The Wells Fargo website is a professional banking platform designed to provide users with access to financial services, including online banking, loans, and investment tools. Its design focuses on usability and accessibility for a wide range of users.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast ensures readability, with bold colors for call-to-action buttons.
  • Repetition: Consistent design elements, such as fonts, colors, and layouts, create a cohesive experience.
  • Alignment: Clean, grid-based alignment gives the website a professional and organized look.
  • Proximity: Logical grouping of related elements and adequate spacing make the site intuitive and user-friendly.

Pacific Northwest X-ray Inc

The Pacific Northwest X-ray Inc website is a platform for medical imaging equipment and supplies. However, its design struggles with usability and accessibility, making it less effective for its intended audience.

  • Contrast: Poor contrast with an overwhelming blue color makes text and elements hard to distinguish.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent design elements, such as fonts and layouts, create a disjointed experience.
  • Alignment: Misaligned elements and a lack of structure result in a cluttered and unprofessional appearance.
  • Proximity: Poor grouping of elements and lack of white space make the site confusing and hard to navigate.
