Interactive Drawing Page

How to Use:

  • Press R to draw in Red.
  • Press G to draw in Green.
  • Press B to draw in Blue.
  • Press W to draw in White.
  • Press C to clear the canvas.
  • Use the Mouse Wheel to adjust the stroke size.
  • Click and drag the mouse to draw on the canvas.
