The Website

Application and its purpose: This website is designed to promote Connor Wetherington's personal fitness brand. It will serve as a platform to showcase his expertise in fitness, provide advice on health and wellness, and connect with his audience through social media and other channels.

Intended users of the website: The primary users of this website are individuals interested in fitness, health, and wellness. This includes athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and people looking for guidance on improving their physical health and lifestyle.

Overview of the content of the website:

  • Home Page: Introduction to Connor and his fitness philosophy.
  • About Page: Detailed information about Connor's background, achievements, and fitness journey.
  • Products Page: Information about fitness products recommended by Connor.
  • Workouts: A page where Connor breaks down some key workouts he followed in order to get in shape.

Wireframe

Site Map

The site map will consist of the following:

  • Home - index.html
  • About
  • Products
  • Workouts

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: The purpose of this page is to introduce Connor and detail what this website will be about.
  • Audience: People that are interested in fitness.
  • Content: A message that greets the viewer, nav bar, and the purpose of the page.
  • Data Input: Nav bar.

About

  • Purpose: To give Connor's fitness story regarding nutrition and detail his achievements within his track career.
  • Audience: People that are interested in Connor and want to replicate his results.
  • Content: Multiple pictures of Connor showing his achievements as well as his physique.
  • Data Input: Nav bar.

Products

  • Purpose: To introduce what supplements Connor takes and where to get them and why he takes certain ones.
  • Audience: People that are interested in achieving their maximum potential through fitness supplements.
  • Content: Pictures of the supplements that Connor takes, their purpose, and what they contain.
  • Data Input: Links for purchase of the supplements.

Workouts

  • Purpose: To break down what workouts Connor does to achieve his physique and a general training plan recommended by him.
  • Audience: Those that are interested in getting in shape and need guidance on where to start.
  • Content: A broken down, easy to read table that gives a weekly schedule of workouts.
  • Data Input: Nav bar.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Form Validation: The about page will have a calorie calculator for users to see how much they need to consume.
  • Slideshow: Users will see a slideshow of Connor with some before and after pictures.
