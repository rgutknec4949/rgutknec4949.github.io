Healthy Meal Ideas

Fuel your body with these nutritious meal options designed to help you perform at your best. Each meal includes macros to help you track your nutrition.

Breakfast

  • Oatmeal with fresh berries and almond butter

    • Macros: 350 calories, 10g protein, 45g carbs, 12g fat

  • Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast

    • Macros: 400 calories, 20g protein, 30g carbs, 15g fat

  • Greek yogurt with granola and honey

    • Macros: 300 calories, 15g protein, 35g carbs, 8g fat

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and avocado

    • Macros: 450 calories, 35g protein, 20g carbs, 25g fat

  • Quinoa bowl with roasted vegetables and hummus

    • Macros: 400 calories, 15g protein, 50g carbs, 12g fat

  • Turkey and spinach wrap with a side of fruit

    • Macros: 350 calories, 25g protein, 40g carbs, 8g fat

Dinner

  • Grilled salmon with steamed broccoli and sweet potato

    • Macros: 500 calories, 40g protein, 35g carbs, 20g fat

  • Lean beef stir-fry with brown rice and vegetables

    • Macros: 450 calories, 35g protein, 40g carbs, 15g fat

  • Baked chicken breast with quinoa and asparagus

    • Macros: 400 calories, 40g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat

Alternative Desserts

  • Dark chocolate-covered almonds

    • Macros: 200 calories, 5g protein, 10g carbs, 15g fat

  • Frozen banana slices dipped in peanut butter

    • Macros: 250 calories, 6g protein, 30g carbs, 10g fat

  • Chia seed pudding with coconut milk and fruit

    • Macros: 300 calories, 8g protein, 25g carbs, 15g fat

Snacks

  • Apple slices with almond butter

    • Macros: 200 calories, 4g protein, 25g carbs, 10g fat

  • Hard-boiled eggs

    • Macros: 140 calories, 12g protein, 1g carbs, 10g fat

  • Carrot sticks with hummus

    • Macros: 150 calories, 5g protein, 15g carbs, 8g fat
