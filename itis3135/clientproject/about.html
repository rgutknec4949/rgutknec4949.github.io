Connor's Fitness Journey

Connor Wetherington is a dedicated decathlete who has spent years perfecting his craft. From running on the track at 7 years old to competing at the division 1 collegiate level, Connor's journey is a testament to hard work, discipline, and passion for fitness.

His training philosophy revolves around balancing strength, endurance, and recovery. Connor believes in pushing limits while maintaining a healthy lifestyle, which has made him a role model for those around him.
