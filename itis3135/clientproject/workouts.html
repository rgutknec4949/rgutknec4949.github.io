4-Week Workout Plan

This program includes 2 lifting days, 3 running days, an active recovery day, and a full rest day each week. Follow this plan to build strength and endurance and also maximize recovery.

Week 1

Day Workout
Monday Upper Body Lifting: Bench Press 5x10 , Pull-Ups 3xMAX, Dumbbell Rows 5x10, Overhead Press 5x10
Tuesday Running: 4x300m w/ 100m walk rest
Wednesday Lower Body Lifting: Squats 5x10, Deadlifts 5x10, Hamstring Curl 5x10, Calf Raises 5x10
Thursday Running: 5x400m w/ 4 min rest in between
Friday Active Recovery: Yoga or light stretching (Optional ice bath)
Saturday Running: Long run (3-4 miles)
Sunday Full Rest

Connor week 1 of the workout program.

Week 2

Day Workout
Monday Upper Body Lifting: Incline Bench Press 5x10, Chin-Ups 3xMAX, Dumbbell Flys 5x10, Arnold Press 5x10
Tuesday Running: 3x800m w/ 5 min rest
Wednesday Lower Body Lifting: Front Squats 5x10, Deadlifts 5x10, Leg Press 5x10, Hamstring Curls 5x10
Thursday Running: 100m hill sprints 20* incline, 2x5
Friday Active Recovery: Foam rolling and mobility work (Optional ice bath, scraping)
Saturday Running: Long run (4-6 miles)
Sunday Full Rest

Connor performing a deadlift during a lifting session.

Week 3 (begin to increase weight)

Day Workout
Monday Upper Body Lifting: Push-Ups 4x25 or Max, Barbell Rows 4x8, Dumbbell Shrugs 4x8, Lateral Raises 5x10
Tuesday Running: 2x1000m w/ 6 min rest
Wednesday Lower Body Lifting: Box Jump 3x10, Squats 4x8, Deadlifts 4x8, Hamstring Curls 4x8, Calf Raises 4x8
Thursday Running: 6x400m w/ 6 min rest
Friday Active Recovery: Swimming or light cycling 30-60 mins
Saturday Running: Long run (5 miles)
Sunday Full Rest

Connor during his rest period in betwen the 400m runs.

Week 4 (increase weight)

Day Workout
Monday Upper Body Lifting: Bench Press 4x8, Pull-Ups 3xMAX, Dumbbell Rows 4x8, Overhead Press 4x8
Tuesday Running: Time trial mile
Wednesday Lower Body Lifting: Squats 4x8, Deadlifts 4x8, Dumbbell Lunges 4x8, Calf Raises 4x8
Thursday Running: 8x200m w/ 100m walk rest
Friday Active Recovery: Yoga or light stretching (Optional ice bath)
Saturday Running: Long run (5 miles)
Sunday Full Rest
